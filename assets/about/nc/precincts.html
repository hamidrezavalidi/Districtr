The units used here are voting tabulation districts or VTDS. Data for this module were obtained from the North Carolina General Assembly and National Historic GIS (NHGIS). The VTD shapefile and election results were downloaded from the North Carolina General Assembly's 2016 Redistricting Reference Data. Demographic information from the 2010 Decennial Census was downloaded at the block level from NHGIS. The source shapefile for this module, along with additional information about processing, is available for download from MGGG's GitHub organization @mggg-states.

In March 2019 the US Supreme Court heard oral arguments for Rucho v. Common Cause, in which plaintiffs claim that North Carolina's remedial 2016 congressional map is an unconstitutional partisan gerrymander. The 2016 remedial map had been drawn after the Supreme Court struck down two of the state's 2011 enacted congressional districts as unconstitutional racial gerrymanders. MGGG and our collaborators filed an amicus brief which can be accessed on our website.
