The units used here are precincts. Data for this module were obtained the City of Chicago Data Portal and from the US Census Bureau. The precinct shapefile was downloaded from the Chicago Data Portal. Demographic information from the 2010 Decennial Census was downloaded at the block level from American FactFinder. The source shapefile with demographic information is available for download from MGGG's GitHub organization.

In April 2019, MGGG published a study on various reform proposals for the Chicago City Council. Our findings strongly support a transition to multimember wards with ranked choice voting to secure and sustain fair representation.
