The units you see here are voting precincts. Data for this module were obtained from the Princeton Gerrymandering Project and National Historic GIS (NHGIS). The precinct shapefile was compiled and joined to election results by the Princeton Gerrymandering Project. Demographic information from the 2010 Decennial Census was downloaded at the block level from NHGIS. The source shapefile for this module, along with additional information about processing, is available for download from MGGG's GitHub organization @mggg-states.

In June 2018, the Eastern District Court of Virginia ruled 11 of the House of Delegates districts unconstitutional racial gerrymanders. MGGG published a report evaluating the 2011 Enacted House of Delegates plan as well as several of the proposed alternatives.
