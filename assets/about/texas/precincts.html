The units used here are voting tabulation districts or VTDS. Data for this module were obtained from the Texas Legislative Council. The VTD shapefile and election results were downloaded from the Texas Legislative Council's Texas Redistricting website. Demographic data were compiled by the Texas Legislative Council using 2010 Decennial Census data. The source shapefile for this module, along with additional information about processing, is available for download from MGGG's GitHub organization @mggg-states.
