The units you see here are precincts. Data for this module were obtained from the Georgia General Assembly Legislative and Congressional Reapportionment Office, the MIT Elections Data and Science Lab (MEDSL), and National Historic GIS (NHGIS). The precinct shapefile for Georgia was downloaded from the Georgia General Assembly Legislative and Congressional Reapportionment Office's website and 2016 election results were downloaded from the MEDSL. Demographic information from the 2010 Decennial Census was downloaded at the block level from NHGIS. The source shapefile for this module, along with additional information about processing, is available for download from MGGG's GitHub organization @mggg-states.
